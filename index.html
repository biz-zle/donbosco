Why Choose Us?

“Choosing DBTHS – Your Path to Excellence and Faith”

At Don Bosco Technical High School, we understand that selecting the right high school is a significant decision for both students and their families. We believe there are compelling reasons why you should choose us as your partner in education and faith:

Faith-Centered Education

We offer more than just academics; we provide an environment where faith and spirituality are integral to the educational experience. Our commitment to Christian values and teachings creates a moral foundation that guides students in making ethical choices and understanding the world through a faith-based lens.

Academic Excellence

With a rigorous academic curriculum, experienced faculty, and a focus on critical thinking and problem-solving, we prepare students for success in higher education and beyond. Our dedication to academic excellence is reflected in our students' achievements, consistently exceeding national and international standards.

Alumni Success Stories

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields, making a positive impact on their communities and the world. They are a testament to the quality education and values instilled at our school. Once you become a part of us, you will always remain family. You can rest assure of joining a community of opportunities.

Forward-Thinking Innovation

We continuously embrace innovation and technology to enhance the learning experience, preparing students for the challenges of the modern world. Our commitment to staying at the forefront of educational advancements ensures students receive the best possible education.

Supportive Community

DBTHS is more than just a school; it's a community of educators, parents, and students working together to foster a supportive and inclusive atmosphere. We offer personalized attention and support, ensuring each student's unique needs are met on their educational journey.

Teaching Methods

The Socratic Method, an amazing teaching approach, centers on open-ended questioning to stimulate critical thinking, self-discovery, and respectful dialogue among students. It fosters deep exploration of complex topics, develops communication skills, and cultivates a lifelong love of learning.
